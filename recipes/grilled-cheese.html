Total Time: 10-13 minutes

Servings: 1 sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread. It's your choice but I recomend sourdough
  • 2-3 Slices each of:
    • havarti
    • gouda
    • cheddar
  • A whole stick of Butter

Instructions

  1. If not already leave butter outside to become room temperature
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat
  3. Spread the butter evenly on one side of the bread
  4. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, onto the skillet
  5. Add each of your cheeses onto that slice of bread. (Order doesn't matter)
  6. Place the 2nd slice of bread on top butter side up
  7. Cook until the bottom is golden brown. Usually 3-4 minutes
  8. Flip the sandwich and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the other side is golden and the cheese has melted
  9. Remove from the skillet and serve!!!

Tips:

  • Add Tomato Slices in the sandwich or some garlic powder on the bread for a more enjoyable experience
